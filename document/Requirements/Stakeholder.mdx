# Stakeholder Requirements

The high level requirements of the two major stakeholders and project delivery partners, are described here, as well as those of the data providers.

|   ID   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | Priority Ranking |
| :----: | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------: |
| MG001  | Reduced clinical variation in best-practice indicators and improved research                                                                                                                                                     |        S         |
| MG002  | Demonstrable use of linked health data to improve care more rapidly (see also SES002)                                                                                                                                            |        M         |
| SES001 | Harness and support research infrastructure to make existing data available for research purposes                                                                                                                                |        M         |
| SES002 | Link SESLHD datasets internally and to other data sources and use these data to explore patient outcomes, clinical variation and other issues of relevance to SESLHD (see also MG002)                                            |        M         |
| SES003 | Develop partnerships with external experts to develop this capability                                                                                                                                                            |        S         |
| SES004 | Implement a research infrastructure sharing scheme across the district                                                                                                                                                           |        M         |
| SES005 | Develop research networks and clinical improvement analytics capacity within quality improvement initiatives                                                                                                                     |        C         |
| DP001  | Data ingested into the data environment must be processed (including, but not limited to, any rules-based transformation, separation of linkage and content data, and storage) in accordance with the policies of the data owner |        M         |
| DP002  | Researcher access to data, including assessment criteria for access, must only be granted in accordance with the policies of the data owner                                                                                      |        M         |
| DP003  | A data dictionary should be created and maintained, and made available to researchers, to help them understand the nature of the data stored in the environment                                                                  |        S         |
| DP004  | Processes and criteria for obtaining access to data must be documented and made available to researchers                                                                                                                         |        S         |
